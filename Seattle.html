Seattle

"The Emerald City"

Seattle is the largest urban city in Washington State with a population of more than 733,904 people. It was incorperated on December 2, 1869 (SMA, 2023). Tha median income for the city is $105,391 (USC, 2023). The Seattle metro area is bordered by the Olympic Mountains to the west across Puget Sound, the Cascade Mountains to the east across Lake Washington, the Salish Sea to the northwest, and British Columbia, Canada to the north. Seattle is a major seaport city and is the seat of King County. It uses the largest ferry system in the US. Seattle is named after Chief Sealth of the Duwamish and Suquamish tribes. Seattle is also the largest city in the Pacific Northwest region of the US. The city is a major gateway for trade with Asia.

Seattle is an amazing city full of incredible attractions, activities, and entertainment. The city is also surrounded by the natural beauty of the Pacific Northwest. The city is home to the Space Needle, the NFL team Seattle Seahawks, the MLB team Seattle Mariners, and the Seattle Sounders FC. Seattle is also home to the Pike Place Market, the first Starbucks, and the Seattle Underground. The major university is the University of Washington.

Washington State is the premiere locale for all things arts, culture, activities, and entertainment!
