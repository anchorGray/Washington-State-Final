Incredible Washington!

"The Evergreen State"

Washington State is the northwestern-most state in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States. It shares borders with Oregon to the south, Idaho to the west, the Pacific Ocean to the west, and British Columbia, Canada to the north. Washinton State is populated by more than 7,000,000 people. Most of the inhabitents live and work in the western part of the state. The majority of rainfall ocurrs in the western areas while the eastern areas recieve substantially less rainfall.

The capital of Washington is Olympia, and the largest city is Seattle. Seattle is also the largest city in the Pacific Northwest region of the US. The state bird is the willow goldfinch, the state flower is the coast rhododendron, and the state tree is the western hemlock. The state nickname is "The Evergreen State" because of its beautiful natural settings with breathtaking forests, mountains, rivers, plains coastline, fjords, and islands (Clark, E. et al, 2023).

Washington State is the premiere locale for all things arts, culture, activities, and entertainment!
