Olympia

"Oly"

The capital city of Washington State is Olympia. It is located at the southern end of Puget Sound. The city is the cultural center of the southern Puget Sound region. It is home to the Evergreen State College, South Puget Sound Community College, and Saint Martin's University, among other insitutions. Olympia is also home to the Olympia Symphony Orchestra, Olympia Film Society, and the Washington Center for the Performing Arts (CO, 2023).

The Washington State Capital Building is a National Historic Landmark. It is the fifth tallest masonry dome in the world and the tallest in North America. The building is made of sandstone and granite. The building is open to the public and tours are available (VTC, 2023).

Washington State is the premiere locale for all things arts, culture, activities, and entertainment!
